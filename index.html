ABOUT THE SONG LYRICS

"Remember This" is a song by the American rapper NF, from his album "Perception," released in 2017.
The song is known for its introspective and reflective lyrics, as is characteristic of NF's style.
In "Remember This," NF reflects on his journey as an artist, the struggles he has faced, and the lessons he has learned along the way.
The lyrics convey themes of perseverance, self-doubt, and the desire for success despite obstacles. Throughout the song, NF addresses his inner demons, expressing his determination to overcome them and achieve his goals.
He also emphasizes the importance of staying true to oneself and not allowing external pressures to dictate one's path in life. "Remember This" serves as a motivational anthem, inspiring listeners to pursue their dreams relentlessly and to remember their worth even in the face of adversity. It has resonated with many fans due to its raw emotion and relatable message.

ABOUT THE ARTIST


NF, born Nathan Feuerstein, emerged as a distinctive voice in the hip-hop scene with his emotionally charged lyrics and introspective storytelling. Growing up in a turbulent environment marked by personal struggles and loss, NF found solace in music, channeling his emotions into his art. His breakout album, "Mansion" (2015), offered a glimpse into his inner world, grappling with themes of mental health, identity, and faith. Tracks like "Intro" and "Mansion" delved into the complexities of his psyche, setting the stage for a career defined by vulnerability and authenticity.
Building on the success of "Mansion," NF continued to captivate audiences with subsequent albums like "Therapy Session" (2016) and "Perception" (2017). These projects showcased NF's evolution as an artist, blending raw honesty with intricate wordplay and dynamic production. Singles such as "Let You Down" and "The Search" catapulted NF into the mainstream, earning him widespread acclaim and commercial success. Through his music, NF has become not only a chart-topping artist but also a beacon of hope for listeners navigating their own struggles, demonstrating the power of resilience and self-expression in the face of adversity.
